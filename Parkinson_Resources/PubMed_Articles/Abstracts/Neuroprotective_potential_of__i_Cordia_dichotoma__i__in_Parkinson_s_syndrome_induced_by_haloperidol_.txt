Title: Neuroprotective potential of <i>Cordia dichotoma</i> in Parkinson's syndrome induced by haloperidol: An animal study.
PMID: 37771955

Abstract:
Parkinson's disease (PD) is one of the major neurodegenerative disorders and the prevalence is expected to increase during the next couple of decades. There is a need for safe and effective therapeutic regimen that can effectively manage this neurotoxicity. The leaves and several other parts of <i>Cordia dichotoma </i>are known to possess number of medicinal properties. The purpose of this study was to examine the neuroprotective role of <i>Cordia dichotoma </i>in an experimental model of haloperidol-induced P.D.
