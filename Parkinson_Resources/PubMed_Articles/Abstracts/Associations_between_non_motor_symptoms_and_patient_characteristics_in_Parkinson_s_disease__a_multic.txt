Title: Associations between non-motor symptoms and patient characteristics in Parkinson's disease: a multicenter cross-sectional study.
PMID: 37744394

Abstract:
Parkinson's disease (PD) is characterized by various non-motor symptoms (NMS), such as constipation, olfactory disturbance, sleep disturbance, mental disorders, and motor symptoms. This study aimed to investigate factors associated with NMS in patients with PD.
