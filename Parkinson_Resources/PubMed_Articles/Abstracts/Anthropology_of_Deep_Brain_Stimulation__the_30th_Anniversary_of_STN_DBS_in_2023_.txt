Title: Anthropology of Deep Brain Stimulation; the 30th Anniversary of STN DBS in 2023.
PMID: 37772285

Abstract:
The year 2023 marks the 30th anniversary of deep brain stimulation (DBS) of the subthalamic nucleus (STN) for Parkinson<b>'</b>s disease (PD). This procedure prompted a universal interest in DBS for various brain disorders and resulted in a unique expansion of clinical and scientific collaboration between many disciplines, with impact on many aspects of society.
