Title: Curcumin, inflammation, and neurological disorders: How are they linked?
PMID: 37664456

Abstract:
Despite the extensive research in recent years, the current treatment modalities for neurological disorders are suboptimal. Curcumin, a polyphenol found in <i>Curcuma</i> genus, has been shown to mitigate the pathophysiology and clinical sequalae involved in neuroinflammation and neurodegenerative diseases.
