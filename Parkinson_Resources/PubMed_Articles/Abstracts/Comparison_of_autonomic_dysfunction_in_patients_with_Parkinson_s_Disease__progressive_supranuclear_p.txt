Title: Comparison of autonomic dysfunction in patients with Parkinson's Disease, progressive supranuclear palsy, and multiple system atrophy.
PMID: 38148738

Abstract:
To assess and compare autonomic nervous system (ANS) dysfunction, especially cardiovascular dysautonomia, in Parkinson's Disease (PD), multiple system atrophy (MSA), progressive supranuclear palsy (PSP), and healthy controls.
