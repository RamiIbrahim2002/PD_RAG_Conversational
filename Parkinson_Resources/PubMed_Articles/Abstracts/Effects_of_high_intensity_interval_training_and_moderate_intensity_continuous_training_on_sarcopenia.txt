Title: Effects of high-intensity interval training and moderate-intensity continuous training on sarcopenia-related parameters in participants with Parkinson's disease: A 24-week randomized pilot trial substudy.
PMID: 37898016

Abstract:
To evaluate the potential efficacy of two different supervised exercise regimens, namely high-intensity interval training (HIIT) and moderate-intensity continuous training (MICT), on sarcopenia-related parameters in participants with Parkinson's disease (PD).
