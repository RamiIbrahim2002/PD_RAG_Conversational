Title: Clinical Manifestations of Subjective Sleep Disorders in Chinese Patients with Parkinson's Disease and Their Relationship with Dopaminergic Drugs.
PMID: 37673041

Abstract:
Sleep disorders are common in Parkinson's disease (PD) and significantly impact quality of life. Herein, we surveyed the incidence and severity of sleep disorders in Chinese PD patients and observed their relationship with dopaminergic drugs.
