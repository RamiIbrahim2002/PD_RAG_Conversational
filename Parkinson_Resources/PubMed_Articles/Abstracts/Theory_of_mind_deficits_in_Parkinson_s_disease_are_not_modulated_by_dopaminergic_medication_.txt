Title: Theory of mind deficits in Parkinson's disease are not modulated by dopaminergic medication.
PMID: 37822526

Abstract:
Patients with Parkinson's disease (PD) exhibit deficits in social cognition, particularly with respect to Theory of Mind (ToM) capacities. It is unclear whether they are associated with PD-related dopamine deficiency and modulated by levodopa replacement therapy.
