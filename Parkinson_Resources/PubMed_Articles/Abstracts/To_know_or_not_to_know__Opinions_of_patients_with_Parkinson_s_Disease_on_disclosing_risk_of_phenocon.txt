Title: To know or not to know? Opinions of patients with Parkinson's Disease on disclosing risk of phenoconversion in RBD.
PMID: 37888900

Abstract:
The aim of our study was to find out the opinion of patients with Parkinson's Disease (PD) whose disease was preceded by REM sleep behaviour disorder (RBD) regarding early information about the high risk of phenoconversion in RBD.
