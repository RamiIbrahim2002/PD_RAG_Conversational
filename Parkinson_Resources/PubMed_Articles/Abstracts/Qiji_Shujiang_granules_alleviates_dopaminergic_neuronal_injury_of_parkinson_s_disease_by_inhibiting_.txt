Title: Qiji Shujiang granules alleviates dopaminergic neuronal injury of parkinson's disease by inhibiting NLRP3/Caspase-1 pathway mediated pyroptosis.
PMID: 37657208

Abstract:
The Qiji Shujiang granule (QJG) is a traditional Chinese drug widely used in treating PD patients. However, the potential mechanism of QJG in PD therapy is still unclear.
