Title: Effect of electroacupuncture on oxidative stress and cell apoptosis induced by ferroptosis in mice with Parkinson's disease.
PMID: 38146247

Abstract:
To observe the effect of electroacupuncture (EA) on ferroptosis and apoptosis-related proteins in the substantia nigra of midbrain in mice with Parkinson's disease (PD), so as to explore its possible mechanisms in the treatment of PD.
