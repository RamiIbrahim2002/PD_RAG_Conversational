Title: Factors influencing the level of stigma in Parkinson's disease in western Turkey.
PMID: 37782059

Abstract:
<p>Stigma is a widespread phenomenon in Parkinson&rsquo;s disease (PD) and has been shown to affect the quality of life of individuals. This study aims to assess the level of stigma and identify the factors contributing to stigma in patients with PD in Turkey.</p>.
