Title: Chronic diseases and multimorbidity patterns, their recent onset, and risk of new-onset Parkinson's disease and related functional degeneration in older adults: a prospective cohort study.
PMID: 37855021

Abstract:
Certain chronic diseases contribute to increased risks of Parkinson's disease (PD), but the association between time-varying multimorbidity patterns and new-onset PD remains underexplored.
