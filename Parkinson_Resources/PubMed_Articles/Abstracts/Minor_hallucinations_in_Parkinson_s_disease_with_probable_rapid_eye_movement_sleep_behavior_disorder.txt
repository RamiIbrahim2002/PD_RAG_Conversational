Title: Minor hallucinations in Parkinson's disease with probable rapid eye movement sleep behavior disorder.
PMID: 37645371

Abstract:
Rapid eye movement sleep behavior disorder (RBD) and minor hallucinations (MHs) are prevalent nonmotor symptoms in Parkinson's disease (PD). The purpose of this study was to explore the association of MHs in PD patients with probable RBD (pRBD).
