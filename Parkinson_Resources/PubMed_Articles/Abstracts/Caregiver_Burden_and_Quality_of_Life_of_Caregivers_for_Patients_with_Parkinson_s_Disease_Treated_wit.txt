Title: Caregiver Burden and Quality of Life of Caregivers for Patients with Parkinson's Disease Treated with Deep Brain Stimulation.
PMID: 37970306

Abstract:
This study aimed to identify caregiver burden (CB) and contributing factors to CB for Parkinson's disease (PD) patients under Subthalamic Nucleus Deep Brain Stimulation (STN-DBS) treatment, and investigation the factors, affecting the caregivers' quality of life (QoL).
