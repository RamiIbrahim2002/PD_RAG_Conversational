Title: Parkinson's disease and comorbid myasthenia gravis: a case report and literature review.
PMID: 38259657

Abstract:
Parkinson's disease (PD) is the second most common neurodegenerative disease after Alzheimer's disease. Myasthenia gravis (MG) is a rare autoimmune disease caused by antibodies against the neuromuscular junction. PD and comorbid MG are rarely seen.
