Title: Cannabidiol and Tetrahydrocannabinol Use in Parkinson's Disease: An Observational Pilot Study.
PMID: 37621812

Abstract:
There is a need for more research examining the use of cannabis, tetrahydrocannabinol (THC), and cannabidiol (CBD) products in people with Parkinson's disease (PD), especially given the recent increase in the use of these products.
